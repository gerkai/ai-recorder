
- Do not manually turn off your screen during transcription. You can dim your screen brightness if you run into battery life issues.
- Place your phone as close to your patient as possible. Excessive background noise may interfere with transcription quality.

- The app is HIPAA compliant, including the transcription, note generating, and note transfer processes, but please exercise caution in how you store and use sensitive PHI information.
